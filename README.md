# androidFTP

## Prerequisites

To use this application, you must enable **USB Debugging** on your Android device.

### How to Enable USB Debugging

1.  **Enable Developer Options**:
    *   Open **Settings** on your Android device.
    *   Navigate to **About Phone** (or **Settings > System > About Phone**).
    *   Find the **Build Number** and tap it **7 times** continuously until you see a message saying "You are now a developer!".

2.  **Enable USB Debugging**:
    *   Go back to the main **Settings** menu.
    *   Navigate to **System > Developer Options** (or just **Developer Options**).
    *   Scroll down to the **Debugging** section.
    *   Toggle on **USB Debugging**.

3.  **Connect Your Device**:
    *   Connect your Android phone to your Mac using a USB cable.
    *   A prompt will appear on your phone asking to "Allow USB debugging?".
    *   Check "Always allow from this computer" and tap **Allow**.
